Cappucino

Description

A traditional Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. The cappuccino is bold yet balanced â€” rich espresso softened by creamy milk and topped with a cloud of frothy foam.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 1/3 cup steamed milk
  • 1/3 cup milk foam
  • Optional garnish: cocoa powder, cinnamon, or nutmeg
  • Classic cappuccino ratio is 1:1:1 -> espresso, steamed milk, foam.

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso
    • Pull 1 shot of espresso
    • Pour into a caapuccino cup (6oz)
  2. Steam the milk
    • Using the steam wand: stretch the milk by introducing air at the beginning, then submerge the want to create silky microfoam
    • Without a machine: heat milk on the stove or microwave, then forth with a handheld frother, whisk, or French press pump
  3. Assemble
    • Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, holding back the foam with a spoon
    • Spoon the milk foam on top to create a fluffy layer
  4. Garnish & Serve
