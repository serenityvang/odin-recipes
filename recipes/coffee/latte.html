Latte

Description

Latte (Hot)

A smooth and creamy espresso-based drink made with rich espresso, steamed milk, and a thin layer of foam. The latte is mellow, comforting, and endlessly customizable with flavored syrups like vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut.

Iced Latte

A refreshing twist on the classic latte â€” espresso poured over chilled milk and ice. Smooth, lightly sweet, and cooling, the iced latte is perfect for warm days and just as customizable with flavored syrups.

Ingredients

Latte (Hot)

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 3/4 cup steamed milk
  • 1-2 tbsp milk foam
  • Optional: flavored syrup (vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, etc.)

Iced Latte

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 3/4 cup cold milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: flavored syrup (vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, etc.)

Instructions

Latte (Hot)

  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso into a large cup 6-12oz
  2. Steam milk until hot and forthy with just a little foam
  3. Pour steamed milk over espresso then add a thing layer of foam on top
  4. Add flavored syrup if desired.

Iced Latte

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice
  2. Brew espresso and let it cool slightly (or pour directly over ice for a stronger taste)
  3. Add milk, stirring to combine.
  4. Add flavored syrup if desired.
