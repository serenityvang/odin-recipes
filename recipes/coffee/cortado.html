Cortado

Description

A Spanish classic, the cortado is a bold yet balanced drink made by “cutting” espresso with an equal amount of steamed milk. Unlike a cappuccino or latte, the milk isn’t frothy but lightly textured, allowing the espresso’s strength to shine through while mellowing its acidity.

Ingredients

  • 1 double shot espresso (60ml)
  • 1-2 oz steamed milk (equal ratio to espresso)

Instructions

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso into a small 4-5 oz glass
  2. Steam milk lightly, just warm and slightly textured, not foamy
  3. Pour the steamed milk directly into the espresso at a 1:1 ratio
  4. Serve in a small glass
