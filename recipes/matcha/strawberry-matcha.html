Ingredients

Matcha Latte

  • 1 tsp Matcha
  • 1/4 cup Warm/Hot Water
  • 3/4 cup milk

Strawberry Syrup

  • 1/2 cup fresh strawberries
  • 2 tbsp sugar (or honey)
  • 1 tbsp water
  • Or use already made strawberry syrup!

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, sift 1 tsp matcha powder (prevents clumps)
  2. Add hot water and whisk in a zig-zag motion until frothy
  3. Once done with the syrup, its best to keep in the fridge for 24 hours before using!
  4. Then sift the strawberry syrup or premade strawberry syrup into the cup, add ice, milk, and then lastly pour your matcha over the top and enjoy!
