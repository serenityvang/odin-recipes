Iced/Hot Matcha Latte

Description

Matcha Latte

A comforting blend of smooth, ceremonial-grade matcha whisked to perfection and steamed milk, creating a creamy, velvety latte with a naturally earthy flavor and gentle sweetness. Warm, calming, and energizing in every sip.

Iced Matcha Latte

Refreshing and vibrant — fine matcha whisked into silky milk and poured over ice for a cool, revitalizing latte. Lightly sweet, earthy, and creamy, it’s the perfect pick-me-up any time of day.

Ingredients

Matcha Latte

  • 1 tsp ceremonial-grade matcha powder
  • 2-3 tbsp hot water
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • Sweetener for taste (honey, simple syrup, vanilla syrup, or sugar)

Iced Matcha Latte

  • 1 tsp ceremonial-grade matcha powder
  • 2-3 tbsp hot water
  • 3/4 cup of cold milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Sweetener to taste (honey, agave, vanilla syrup, or sugar)

Instructions

Matcha Latte

  1. Whisk the matcha: Sift 1 tsp of matcha into a bowl. Add hot water and whisk until smooth and frothy
  2. Heat the milk: Steam or gently warm the milk until hot but not boiling. Froth if you'd like a cafe-style texture
  3. Combine: Pour the matcha into a mug, add sweetner then slowly pour in the steamed milk.
  4. Serve: Top with a light foam for that latte finish

Iced Matcha Latte

  1. Whisk the matcha: Sift 1 tsp matcha into a bowl. Add hot water and whisk until smooth and frothy
  2. Prepare the glass: Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.
  3. Assemble: Pour the cold milk over the ice, stir in your sweetner, then gently pour in the whisked matcha for a layered effect
  4. Stir before drinking for a smooth, creamy balance.
