Yuzu Cream Top Matcha Latte

Description

Made with a hint of honey 🐝 Inspired by Hokkaido milk ice cream and yuzu desserts had on a hot summer day in Japan 🐏🌻

Ingredients

Matcha Latte

  • 3g Matcha
  • 6g Sugar
  • 70g Hot Water
  • 0.5 cup of milk

Yuzu Honey Cream Top

  • 1 cup Heavy whipping cream
  • 0.5 tbsp Yuzu Super Juice
  • 15g sugar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk your Matcha with Hot Water and set aside
  2. Combine cream, sugar, yuzu juice, honey and salt in a bowl. Use an electric hand mixer (or whisk) until you create the desired consistency
  3. Pour matcha into a cup over ice. Add milk. Top with the Yuzu Honey Cream Top
  4. Add sugar to desire sweetness
