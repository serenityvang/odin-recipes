Raspberry Matcha Latte

Description

Raspberry Matcha Latte is perfect for the warmer months or any time you're craving something fruity than the usual matcha latte. For the Raspberry portion itself of the drink, you could use already made syrup or make the syrup yourself

Ingredients

Matcha Latte

  • 2tsp Matcha
  • 70g warm Water
  • 0.5 cup of milk

Raspberry Syrup

  • Raspberry
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Or use already made raspberry syrup!

Instructions

  1. Whisk your Matcha with warm water and set aside
  2. If you are creating your own raspberry syrup, add water, raspberry, and sugar into a pot and heat it up and smash ingredients to make the syrup.
  3. Once done with the syrup, its best to keep in the fridge for 24 hours before using!
  4. Then sift the raspberry syrup or premade raspberry syrup into the cup, add ice, milk, and then lastly pour your matcha over the top and enjoy!
